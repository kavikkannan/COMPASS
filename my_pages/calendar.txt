# CALENDAR PAGE - Detailed Description

## Page Overview
The Calendar page provides comprehensive Google Calendar integration specifically designed for legal professionals. It features a full-featured calendar interface with specialized legal event types, event management capabilities, and seamless Google Calendar synchronization. The page is optimized for managing legal schedules, court dates, deadlines, and meetings.

## Calendar Display

### Event Types and Color Coding
The calendar supports four specialized legal event types with distinct color coding:

1. **Hearing** (Blue #2563eb)
   - Court hearings and legal proceedings
   - Most critical legal events
   - High priority visual treatment

2. **Meeting** (Green #16a34a)
   - Client meetings and consultations
   - Internal team meetings
   - Professional networking events

3. **Deadline** (Red #dc2626)
   - Filing deadlines and due dates
   - Critical time-sensitive events
   - Urgent attention required

4. **General** (Gray #64748b)
   - Miscellaneous legal events
   - General appointments
   - Non-specific legal activities
